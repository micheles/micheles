Zope experience


Literal quote from the PyCON doctest talk:
  • ~ 5600 tests (~3500 in Zope 3, ~1300 in ZODB, ~800 in Zope 2)
  • we wrote lots of tests before we knew what we were doing

  • most new tests are doctest based
